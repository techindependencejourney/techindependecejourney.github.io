Search Engines

Whether you like it or not, Google tracks you even when your using their search engine. Your data and search on other sites is sold to third parties to target ads against you.

1: DuckDuckGo

DuckDuckGo is the most mainstream search engine related browser, and although it is not the best, it is still a great search engine for privacy.

Here are the pros:

  1. Privacy-respective
  2. No tracking
  3. Has bangs

Although these are sweet pros, DuckDuckGo has some cons:

  1. Partened with Amazon (servers) and Microsoft (search results)
  2. Not Open Source
